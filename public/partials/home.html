About

ProjectBeta is the IT club of Sanskriti School.

Taking its humble roots as a small group of talented individuals in 2013, ProjectBeta has since matured into a diverse team who have worked in tandem to inspire, organise and lead countless events and competitions to success.

Constituting 40+ incredibly talented members the club has represented the school in various competitions and has grown to become one of the foremost clubs of its kind in New Delhi.
